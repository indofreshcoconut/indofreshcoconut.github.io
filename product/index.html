Product

Indo Fresh Coconut exclusively offers the most properly selected Fresh Mature Coconut, having weight general around 0.8 – 1.1 kg per piece

SEMI HUSKED COCONUTS

Totally full grown coconuts along with the husk are kept intact around the “meat” of the coconut, the endosperm. Allowing the husk untouched raises the shelf lifetime of the coconut, guarding the sensitive area to decay.

THE SORTING

Our fresh mature coconuts arrive primarily from Central Java. Those coconuts are picked for their larger size and thicker “meat” for more satisfaction.

Right after harvesting, these top quality coconuts are peeled off and transferred to our local storage facility. We preserve our coconuts fresh by keeping them in a partially open storage place, which there is an air circulation, still shielded from rainwater
Then These coconuts are sorted out for the 0.8 Kg up super quality, or based to customer’s specific demand. Our regular quality selecting delivers semi husked coconuts.

Afterwards, the coconuts are delivered to our main warehouses for packaging. Next our specialists finish the last quality check before delivering them for shipment.
